Chapter 03
The Playbook

How I think. How I lead.
How I deliver.

A consulting-grade delivery mindset applied at every phase of the game.

1
Phase 01 · Pre-Game
Before Delivery
Structure the problem. Align before starting.
  • Define success criteria before writing the first line in a project plan
  • Map stakeholders by influence, not just hierarchy
  • Surface hidden assumptions and constraints in discovery
  • Build a risk register before the risk register is asked for
  • Agree on what "done" means — in writing
2
Phase 02 · Match Day
During Delivery
Create momentum. Remove friction. Communicate relentlessly.
  • Hold teams to commitments without micromanaging the how
  • Convert blockers into decisions within 24 hours
  • Make status visible — no surprises at governance reviews
  • Run retrospectives that actually change behaviour
  • Protect team focus from scope creep and executive noise
3
Phase 03 · Post-Match
After Go-Live
Measure adoption. Capture learning. Close properly.
  • Run structured hypercare — not "fix it as they come"
  • Track adoption metrics, not just go-live dates
  • Document lessons learned while memory is fresh
  • Ensure business owners can sustain what was delivered
  • Close the loop — confirm value was realised

Chapter 07
Game Rules

How I think. How I lead.

🎯
Rule 01
Outcomes over outputs
A shipped feature means nothing if it isn't adopted. I measure success in behaviour change and business results — not go-live dates.
🔬
Rule 02
Hypothesis-driven, not assumption-driven
Before I solve a problem, I name the assumptions I'm making. It saves weeks of work and keeps teams honest about what we know versus believe.
🌉
Rule 03
I translate, not just manage
Strategy without execution is a slide deck. Execution without strategy is chaos. I live in the gap — making both sides coherent to each other.
🚀
Rule 04
Bias to action, not perfection
The best plan at 80% clarity that gets started beats the perfect plan that never leaves the whiteboard. I move, learn, and adjust — in that order.
